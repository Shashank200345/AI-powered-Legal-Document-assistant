RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is made this 15th day of January, 2024, between John Smith, an individual ("Landlord") and Jane Doe, an individual ("Tenant").

PROPERTY DESCRIPTION
Landlord hereby leases to Tenant the residential property located at 123 Main Street, Apartment 2B, Anytown, State 12345 ("Premises").

TERM
The term of this lease shall be for one (1) year, commencing on February 1, 2024 and ending on January 31, 2025, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions herein.

RENT
Tenant shall pay to Landlord monthly rent in the amount of Two Thousand Dollars ($2,000.00), due on the first day of each month. Rent payments received after the 5th day of the month shall incur a late fee of Fifty Dollars ($50.00) per day until paid in full.

SECURITY DEPOSIT
Tenant has deposited with Landlord the sum of Four Thousand Dollars ($4,000.00) as security for the faithful performance of Tenant's obligations hereunder. This deposit may be applied by Landlord to cure any default by Tenant or to compensate Landlord for any damages sustained by reason of Tenant's occupancy.

TERMINATION
This lease may be terminated by either party with thirty (30) days written notice. In the event of breach of any provision of this Agreement by Tenant, Landlord may terminate this lease immediately upon written notice. If Tenant vacates the premises before the expiration of the lease term without Landlord's consent, Tenant shall remain liable for all rent due through the end of the lease term.

USE OF PREMISES
The Premises shall be used solely as a private residence for Tenant and Tenant's immediate family. No business or commercial activities shall be conducted on the Premises without prior written consent of Landlord.

MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
Tenant shall maintain the Premises in good condition and shall be responsible for all repairs and damages caused by Tenant's negligence or misuse. Landlord shall be responsible for structural repairs and maintenance of major systems including plumbing, electrical, and HVAC.

LIABILITY
Tenant assumes all liability for injuries or property damage occurring on or about the Premises during the term of this lease, except for damages caused by Landlord's negligence. Tenant agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from any claims, damages, or expenses arising from Tenant's use of the Premises.

PETS
No pets are allowed on the Premises without prior written consent of Landlord. Any unauthorized pets will result in immediate termination of this lease and forfeiture of security deposit.

SUBLETTING
Tenant may not sublet the Premises or assign this lease without the prior written consent of Landlord, which consent may be withheld in Landlord's sole discretion.

INSURANCE
Tenant is strongly advised to obtain renter's insurance to protect personal property and provide liability coverage.

GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State in which the Premises are located.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement on the date first written above.
